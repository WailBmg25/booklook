# BookLook Product Overview

BookLook is a big data book library application that allows users to browse books, manage reading progress, write reviews, and maintain favorite lists.

## Core Features

- **Book Management**: Browse, search, and filter books by genre, rating, and other criteria
- **User Accounts**: Registration, authentication, and profile management
- **Reading Progress**: Track current page and reading status for books
- **Reviews & Ratings**: Users can rate and review books with sentiment analysis
- **Favorites**: Users can maintain a list of favorite books
- **Caching**: Redis-based caching for improved performance

## Target Users

Readers who want to track their reading habits, discover new books, and share reviews with others.
